Grilled Cheese

Description

A magnificent recipe for a grilled cheese sandwhich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon of butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place one slice of bread, butter-side down, in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Top with cheese and salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Place the other slice of bread, butter-side up, on top of the cheese.
  5. Cook until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown, about 2 minutes.
  6. Flip the sandwich and cook until the other slice of bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 2 minutes more.
  7. Cut the sandwich in half and serve immediately.
