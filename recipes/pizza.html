Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 (12-inch) pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Spread pizza sauce on the crust.
  3. Top with mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
