Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup uncooked egg noodles

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion, carrots, and celery and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the thyme, basil, salt, and pepper and cook for 1 minute more.
  4. Add the chicken broth, cream of chicken soup, chicken, and egg noodles and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes, or until the noodles are cooked through.
  6. Serve hot.
